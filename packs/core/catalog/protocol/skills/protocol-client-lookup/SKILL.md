# protocol-client-lookup - pull a client's real record

Bring the client's current Protocol data into the conversation before reasoning about them.

## When to use

- The operator names a client and asks how they're doing, what they're on, or what to change.

## Steps

1. Find the client in Protocol and open their current program, recent check-ins, and progress.
2. Summarize where they are: adherence, recent trend, anything flagged.
3. Surface anything that needs the coach's attention (missed check-ins, plateaus, messages).
4. Offer the next action (adjust program, message them) - see protocol-build-program.

## Rules

- Reading a client's record is safe; **changing programs or messaging** waits for approval.
- Prefer real, round, practical numbers; never invent progress the record doesn't show.
