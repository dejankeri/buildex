# weekly-review - a weekly pulse from the brain

Produces a short, honest weekly review by reading what actually changed in the workspace and its
connected sources, not by asking people to write status updates.

## When to use

- The start or end of a week, or before a leadership check-in.
- The team wants to see progress and blockers without a meeting.

## Steps

1. Read the last week of commits, new `sources/` material, and the decision log.
2. Draft: **Shipped**, **In progress**, **Blocked / needs a decision**, **Notable inbound**.
3. Link each point to the doc or thread it came from.
4. Save to `reviews/<year>-w<week>.md` and surface anything needing a human decision.

## Rules

- Honest over flattering - surface what's stuck, not just wins.
- Everything cites its source; no unsupported claims.
- Read-only over the sources; drafting a review never sends anything outward.
