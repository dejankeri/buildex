Questions

The things a careful founder asks.

Do you see our data or our AI credentials?+
No. The brain lives on your machine and syncs to a repo you control. Your agent authenticates itself with your own subscription - BuildEx never sets or reads your model keys. That's an architectural invariant, not a promise.
What exactly do we own at the end?+
A repository of plain markdown: every decision, client, loop and convention. Clone it and it runs anywhere. There is no proprietary format and nothing to rip out.
How is this safe if an AI is operating the company?+
The agent reads freely and drafts freely, but anything outward or irreversible - sending, paying, posting, deleting - waits in a Pending tray for a human tap. Autonomy is something the gate grants, loop by loop, as trust builds.
Which AI does it use?+
Claude Code today; the driver seam is open - other agents welcome as contributions. It requires a Claude Pro subscription or higher. We never resell you tokens.
Is this just for technical companies?+
No. The files are plain text, but you never see them - the console speaks in companies, clients and approvals, not repos and terminals. It's built for founders and operators, not developers.
What does it cost?+
The app is free and open source under MIT - download it and run it yourself, forever. For teams, we offer hosted sync and seats. Tell us about your company and we'll be straight about the details.
